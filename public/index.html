How It Works

A personalised story in three simple steps

No writing skills needed. No waiting. Just your child's details and a sprinkle of AI magic.

👶1

Tell us about your child

Enter their name, age, and a favourite interest. Choose a story theme. Under a minute, no sign-up needed to preview.

✨2

AI creates the story

Our AI writes a unique, age-appropriate adventure with your child as the hero — vivid characters and a meaningful ending.

📖3

Read, earn, and explore

Your child reads, answers comprehension questions, and earns Bee points to unlock new adventures and accessories.

🐝4

Watch the habit form

Daily reading streaks, Bee rank promotions, and personalised reminders keep children coming back — without the bedtime fight.

Features

Everything children need to fall in love with reading

Built for parents, designed for children, and trusted by UK primary schools.

🎭

Your child is always the hero

Every story is written around your child's name, appearance, and interests. Not a name-swap — a truly personalised adventure.

🐝

Bee Reward System

Children earn Bees for reading, answering questions, and maintaining streaks. Ten rank levels from Solitary Bee to Queen Bee.

🌍

Bilingual stories

Generate the same story in English and Spanish side by side. French coming soon. Cultural nuance preserved — not just translated.

📴

Offline reading

Stories download automatically on creation. Perfect for car journeys and places with no internet. Syncs Bees when you reconnect.

🧠

Comprehension questions

Three age-appropriate questions after every story — the only measurable literacy proof point, visible in your parent dashboard.

♿

SEN-friendly mode

OpenDyslexic font, high-contrast display, and natural-voice narration enabled by default for SEN profiles.

📊

Parent dashboard

See exactly what your child has read, words encountered, and how comprehension is improving — week by week.

🔒

Child PIN mode

Your child gets their own locked reading screen with a 4-digit PIN. No access to billing, settings, or your account — ever.

🎁

Story gift cards

Give the gift of a personalised story experience. No account needed to purchase. Perfect for birthdays and celebrations.
